Women in Cryptography (WinC)

This site is dedicated to the 'Women in Cryptography' community, inspired by the Women in Number Theory. We aim to be a resource both to women cryptographers but also to community as a whole, by showcasing the many contributions of women cryptographers to the discipline. Contact us if you want to get in touch!
